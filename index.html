The vision

If you have ever tried to organize a potluck through text messages, online to-do lists or spreadsheets you get why this app is important. In the world of social gatherings and potlucks the "Potluck Planner" is king. This is your place for all things pot luck.

Meet the team

From diverse places but with common interest, we are all here for the love of Potlucks. With years of frustration and never being able to successfully plan and implement an awesome potluck, we share a common goal and passion, making this application the best for you and your potluck needs.
